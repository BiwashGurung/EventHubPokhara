About Us

Who We Are

We are EventHubPokhara, dedicated to curating and showcasing the best events happening in Pokhara. From concerts to workshops, we connect you with unforgettable experiences. Our team is passionate about bringing the community together through cultural and artistic events.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make event booking seamless and enjoyable. We aim to support local artists and event organizers, creating a vibrant cultural scene in Pokhara. We believe that by fostering a strong sense of community through events, we can contribute to the cultural and social enrichment of our city.
